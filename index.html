The ACAB Map was a collaborative project collecting "ACAB / 1312" tags in public spaces across Europe.

From 2018 to 2024, the map documented over 1,600 tags, serving as a visual archive of a particular form of urban expression.

This project is presented for documentary and research purposes only. It does not promote or condone vandalism, nor does it endorse the messages depicted.
